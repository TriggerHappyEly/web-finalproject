With the largest selection, best prices, and friendly and knowledgable staff - Casa Del Humidor is the premiere destination for cigars, tobacco, and accessories. And with a clean and comfortable year-round patio, it is also the place for you and your friends to gather, unwind, and enjoy a moment.

Truly Great Cigars

With the largest walk-in humidor in Canada you simply will not find a more comprehensive selection of cigars anywhere else.

Our staff is constantly researching and importing new and exciting products from Cuba, Columbia, Nicaragua, and across the globe for you to enjoy.

Our four-season outdoor patio is conveniently located on Rama Road and allows you to truly enjoy your new found discoveries with an esspresso, coffee, latte, Americano, or nearly any other coffee you desire.

Casa Del Humidor is the perfect stage for your friends to gather and enjoy each other's company while enjoying a world-class cigar.
